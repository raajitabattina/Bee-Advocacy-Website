Why are bees important?

Bees are crucial pollinators in the food supply chain, but they are endangered due to habitat loss, pesticide use, and climate change. Saving bees is essential for food security.

Statistics

2021 Honey Bee Colonies Loss Estimates

  • Summer loss: 30%
  • Winter loss: 30% - 40%
  • Annual loss: >40%
